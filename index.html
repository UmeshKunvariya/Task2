Swami Vivekananda

1863 - 1902

"Arise! Awake! And stop not until the goal is reached."

Biography

"He was a spiritual leader and social reformer. His lectures, writings, letters, poems, and ideas motivated not only the youth of India but also the whole world. Swami Vivekananda is credited with introducing the West to the Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga. He worked in the field of social service. He spoke to people in India and urged them to eliminate the caste system and promote science and industrialisation.He was a figure with high thinking and simple living"
